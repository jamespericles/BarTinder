About BarTinder

BarTinder began as a passion project in many ways. Prior to learning web-development, I was a bartender with a passion for serving up great drinks. I created this project to set myself apart from my competition but to also provide users with a convenient place to find drinks they'll love.


BarTinder brings the drink selection to you. You're able to keep stock of your inventory while BarTinder does all the heavy lifting for you. That means we'll consider what ingredients you have and serve up recipes you can make.


How It Works

BarTinder is as simple as it gets. Add all your ingredients into "My Bar": liquor, bitters, juices, and syrups. BarTinder will let you know what you can make and update your inventory with each drink made.


My Bar

First things first, take a peek inside your liquor cabinet and fridge. Add what ingredients you have and we'll store them for you.

Recipe Selection

Start looking through what cocktails you can enjoy. As you drink, each cocktail ingredient will be taken from "My Bar".

Ease of Use

BarTinder takes the guesswork out of drink making, streamlining the entire process of at home bartending.

Save Recipes for Later

If you find a cocktail you love, you can save it for later. That way you can always find it, regardless of what ingredients you have.

Search for Something

If you'd prefer, you can search for a specific cocktail as well, regardless of if you have the ingredients or not.

Creating an account will help you take your bar anywhere, recipes included!
